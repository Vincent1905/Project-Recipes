Pizza

Description

This beautifully handcrafted Italian pizza was made by Gordan Ramsay himself, using the most robust tomato sauce and top notch ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Green Peppers
  • Pepperoni
  • Sausage
  • Black Olives
  • Bread
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Create nice bread
  2. Create tomato sauce and lay it on bread
  3. Melt cheese over the tomato sauce
  4. Lay the rest of the toppings over the cheese

    5. Congratulations, you now have yummy pizza!
